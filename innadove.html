Frequently Asked
Questions

01 What is the typical payment structure for an interior design project?

Payment is usually divided into several stages:
50% before the start of work,
30% after the layout is approved,
20% after the final documentation is delivered.

This helps distribute the financial load evenly and ensures transparent cooperation.

02 At what stage should you involve an interior designer?

Ideally before renovation or construction begins.
This helps avoid rework and plan the placement of sockets, lighting, and plumbing in advance, as well as build partitions according to the approved layout.

03 Can I order only part of the project?

Yes. We offer flexible formats from layout and moodboards to full project packages with drawings and supervision.

04 How long does project development take?

On average 5 to 8 weeks for apartments up to 100 m², depending on complexity and approvals.

05 How do you work with remote clients?

We work online using measurements, photos, videos, and video calls, delivering all materials digitally.

06 What if the project scope changes?

We adjust the budget and timeline accordingly.

07 Do you help with furniture and materials?

Yes. We provide real product lists with store links.

08 What will I receive at the end of the project?

Layouts, 3D visuals, technical drawings, and material specifications.

09 How many revision rounds are included in the price?

Typically, up to 3 revision rounds are included at each stage. This is more than enough to find the ideal solution. If additional changes are needed, we agree on them separately, always with a flexible approach.

10 What role does design supervision play?

Design supervision is control over the project implementation. We coordinate contractors, check drawings on site, and resolve day-to-day questions. This ensures the concept is executed exactly as shown in the visualizations.

11 What are the client’s responsibilities?

The key responsibility is providing input data and feedback on time. We support you at every stage, but decisions and approvals remain with you. When the process is a dialogue, the project moves smoothly and comfortably.

12 Do you offer any guarantees or support after the project is completed?

Yes. After completion, you can always contact us with questions during implementation. We stay in touch after delivery and help if clarifications or adjustments are needed.

13 How do you define the interior style?

We don’t impose a style — we discover it together. In the first meeting we discuss your preferences, lifestyle, and the atmosphere you want to create. The goal is an interior that reflects you, not trends. If choosing a style is difficult, we provide references of current directions and analyze them together to define the right path.

14 In which countries do you work?

We deliver projects in Poland, Ukraine, Germany, Spain, and other countries across Europe and worldwide. For remote projects we work online, and when needed, we travel for design supervision.
